When you purchase a Timeless Aston Martin, you can rest assured that your Aston Martin has been thoughtfully curated to the highest showroom standards by skilled Aston Martin technicians. Available on vehicles up to 10 years old, Timeless Certified-Preowned vehicles come with a variety of special benefits.

ARRIVE IN STYLE

Timeless owners receive a 15% discount* on eligible Aston Martin Accessories for three months following the purchase date of your Aston Martin.

12 MONTHS EXTENDED WARRANTY

Every Timeless purchase includes a minimum of 12 months unlimited mileage warranty**. Available exclusively from Aston Martin Tampa Bay, you can be confident in the knowledge that your Aston Martin will be repaired at an official Aston Martin service center by factory trained technicians and using genuine Aston Martin parts. This coverage can be easier renewed when existing coverage concludes and is transferable, enhancing the resale value of your Aston Martin.

12 MONTHS ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE

As part of your purchase process, you will receive the details for Aston Martin roadside assistance should you ever require their support within the first 12 months of ownership.

CERTIFIED HISTORY AND MILEAGE

All vehicles come with an independently certified history and verified mileage (subject to the availability of data). You’ll drive away with the knowledge that your new Timeless vehicle has a complete history and delivers what you expected.

QUALITY ASSURED ​ASTON MARTIN GENUINE PARTS

At Aston Martin Tampa Bay, we guarantee to use only genuine parts backed by a 12-month warranty. Nothing less is acceptable to our high standards, and every single component of your car has been designed, tested and fine-tuned to deliver the optimum Aston Martin driving experience.

MULTI-POINT PRE-DELIVERY HARDWARE & SOFTWARE CHECK

Factory trained technicians complete the Multi-Point Timeless Inspection on all approved pre-owned Aston Martin’s. This detailed examination of all components ensures that your vehicle meets our exacting requirements, consistent with the age and mileage of the vehicle. Multi-point inspection items include the vehicle exterior, interior, major mechanical components, safety and security checks, wheels and tires, vehicle history, engine, transmission, suspension, steering components and a thorough test drive to ensure that your Aston Martin meets all of the Timeless quality standards.

AM MAGAZINE SUBSCRIPTION

Timeless owners enjoy a complimentary 12-month subscription to AM Magazine which includes three issues. Your AM subscription allows you to uncover the extraordinary lifestyle that comes with owning an Aston Martin and highlights the latest news on new models, and people, places and brands that share a synergy with Aston Martin’s values.
